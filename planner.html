About Virtual Vectors

Virtual Vectors was born from a passion for realistic flight simulation. I saw a need for a dedicated, powerful tool that allows virtual air traffic controllers to plan and visualize complex approach patterns with ease and precision. The planner is designed to be intuitive for beginners while offering the depth and flexibility demanded by seasoned enthusiasts. I am committed to providing a high-quality, free-to-use tool for the entire community.

Features

Interactive Global Map

Load and explore thousands of airports worldwide on a dynamic and responsive map interface, complete with multiple terrain and map style options.

Detailed Airport Information

Access crucial airport data, including runway layouts, true headings, elevation, and airspace class, to inform your control strategy.

Intuitive Drawing Tool

Draw, edit, and manage flight paths directly on the map. Create standard, arrival, or departure legs with live heading and distance feedback. Please note: All headings provided by the tool are **True Headings**.

Flight Plan Management

Organize your flight paths into a comprehensive plan. Assign and edit altitudes, speeds, and leg types for full control over your sequence.

Navaid & Waypoint Integration

Display VORs on the map to build realistic, procedure-based routes. Full waypoint integration is a planned feature for the near future.

Altitude Profile Editor

Fine-tune the vertical profile of any flight leg with a graphical editor, allowing for precise control over descents and climbs.
